# Compliance Policy

**Last Updated: January 2024**

## Regulatory Compliance

WinSnip is committed to operating in full compliance with applicable laws and regulations across all jurisdictions where we provide services.

## Legal Framework

### Jurisdictional Compliance

- **Indonesia**: Primary jurisdiction of operation
- **International**: Compliance with international blockchain regulations
- **Data Protection**: GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations
- **Financial Regulations**: AML/KYC where applicable

### Licensing

- Operating under appropriate licenses and registrations
- Regular compliance reviews and updates
- Cooperation with regulatory authorities
- Transparent business practices

## Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

### AML Program

We maintain a comprehensive AML program:

- **Risk Assessment**: Regular risk-based assessments
- **Customer Due Diligence**: Appropriate verification procedures
- **Transaction Monitoring**: Automated monitoring systems
- **Suspicious Activity Reporting**: Timely reporting to authorities

### Know Your Customer (KYC)

For certain services, we may require:

- Identity verification
- Proof of address
- Source of funds documentation
- Enhanced due diligence for high-risk customers

### Prohibited Activities

We prohibit use of our services for:

- Money laundering
- Terrorist financing
- Fraud or illegal activities
- Sanctions evasion
- Any unlawful purposes

## Sanctions Compliance

### Sanctions Screening

- Screening against OFAC, UN, and EU sanctions lists
- Blocking access from sanctioned jurisdictions
- Monitoring for sanctioned entities
- Regular sanctions list updates

### Restricted Jurisdictions

Services may not be available in certain jurisdictions due to:

- Legal restrictions
- Regulatory requirements
- Sanctions programs
- Risk assessments

## Data Protection Compliance

### GDPR Compliance

For European users:

- **Legal Basis**: Legitimate processing grounds
- **Data Rights**: Access, correction, deletion, portability
- **Data Protection Officer**: Designated DPO contact
- **Data Transfers**: Appropriate safeguards for international transfers

### CCPA Compliance

For California residents:

- Right to know what data is collected
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of data sales
- Non-discrimination for exercising rights

### Other Privacy Laws

Compliance with privacy laws including:

- Indonesia's data protection regulations
- Other applicable regional privacy laws
- Industry-specific privacy requirements
