# Voting Guide

Learn how to vote on governance proposals.

## How to Vote

### Step 1: Connect Wallet
- Ensure wallet is connected
- Must have staked tokens to vote

### Step 2: View Proposals
1. Go to Proposals page
2. Find active proposals
3. Click to view details

### Step 3: Cast Vote
1. Click "Vote" button
2. Choose: Yes, No, Abstain, or Veto
3. Confirm in wallet

## Vote Options
- **Yes**: Support the proposal
- **No**: Against the proposal
- **Abstain**: Neutral, counts for quorum
- **Veto**: Strong opposition

## Voting Power
Your voting power = Your staked tokens

## Important
- Can only vote during voting period
- Cannot change vote after submission
- Validators can vote on behalf of delegators
- Delegators can override validator vote
