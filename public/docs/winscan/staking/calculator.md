# Staking Calculator

Calculate your potential staking rewards and plan your investment strategy.

## What is the Staking Calculator?

The Staking Calculator helps you estimate potential rewards from staking your tokens. It considers factors like APR, commission rates, and compounding frequency.

## How to Use

### Step 1: Access Calculator
1. Go to any chain on WinScan
2. Click **"Staking Calculator"** in the sidebar
3. Calculator interface will appear

### Step 2: Enter Details
- **Amount**: How many tokens to stake
- **APR**: Annual Percentage Rate (auto-filled)
- **Commission**: Validator commission rate
- **Period**: How long to stake (days/months/years)

### Step 3: View Results
- Daily rewards
- Monthly rewards
- Annual rewards
- Total after period

## Example Calculation

```
Stake Amount: 1,000 PAXI
APR: 15%
Commission: 5%
Period: 1 year

Results:
Daily: 0.39 PAXI
Monthly: 11.88 PAXI
Yearly: 142.5 PAXI
Total: 1,142.5 PAXI
```

## Compound vs Simple Interest

### Simple Interest
Rewards are not re-staked:
```
Year 1: 1,000 + 150 = 1,150 PAXI
Year 2: 1,000 + 150 = 1,150 PAXI (same)
```

### Compound Interest
Rewards are re-staked monthly:
```
Year 1: 1,000 → 1,161 PAXI
Year 2: 1,161 → 1,348 PAXI
```

**Difference**: 198 PAXI more with compounding!

## Tips for Maximum Returns

1. **Compound Regularly**: Claim and redelegate rewards monthly
2. **Choose Low Commission**: 5% vs 10% = 5% more rewards
3. **High Uptime Validators**: 99.9% uptime = consistent rewards
4. **Long-term Staking**: Compound effect increases over time
