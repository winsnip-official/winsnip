# IBC Relayers

Information about IBC relayers and their activity.

## What are Relayers?

Relayers are services that facilitate IBC transfers by relaying packets between chains.

## Features
- Active relayers list
- Channel information
- Transfer statistics
- Relayer performance

## How to View
1. Click "Relayers" in sidebar
2. See all active relayers
3. View channel details

## Relayer Info
- Relayer name
- Channels operated
- Total transfers
- Success rate
- Last activity

## Why Important?
- Enable cross-chain transfers
- Maintain channel health
- Process IBC packets
- Connect ecosystems
